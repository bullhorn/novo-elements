🎉 March 2026 (version 12)
===========================

**Announcement**: Novo Elements is now on Angular 20!

This brings Novo Elements up to recent versions of both Angular and Typescript.

## Upgrading to v12

- Ensure your node version is compatible with Angular 20 which requires `^20.19.0` or `^22.12.0` or `^24.0.0`.
